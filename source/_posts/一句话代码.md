# 正则

## 按照xxx-xxxx-xxxx匹配电话号码

如15000000000,需要变成150-0000-0000
```
num.replace(/(\d)(!=(\d{4}+)\b)/),'$&-')
// or
num.replace(/(\B)(?=(\d{4})+(?!\d))/,'-')
```
